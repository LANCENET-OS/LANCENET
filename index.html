Subject: The Fall of Handlers

How LANCENET Rendered Handlers Obsolete

Once considered an integral part of Lancer operations, Handlers managed mission requests, contracts, and communications between teams and their employers. But with the rise of LANCENET, many Lancer teams began opting for a more autonomous approach. The introduction of LANCENET by The Research Court and Boundary Benders allowed Lancers to handle their own missions and requests without the need for Handlers, ultimately leading to their downfall.

The system works. Its efficient, says one Lancer who transitioned early to LANCENET. I dont need someone middle-manning my contracts anymore. With this shift, many Handlers found themselves out of work, while others were reassigned to less vital roles. Despite some resistance from traditionalist teams, LANCENET became the new standard.
